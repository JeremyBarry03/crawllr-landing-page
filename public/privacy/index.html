Crawllr Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated January 2026

This policy explains what information Crawllr collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Crawllr you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account details you provide, such as email, profile data, and photos.
  • Content you share in the app, including crawls, plans, and messages.
  • Usage data like app interactions, device type, and log information.
  • Location data if you enable location features on your device.

How we use your information

  • Operate and improve Crawllr and keep the service reliable.
  • Provide customer support and communicate about your account.
  • Personalize your experience and keep the community safe.

Sharing and disclosure

We share information with trusted service providers who help us run Crawllr and only as required to operate the app. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our users. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention and security

We keep data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. We use reasonable safeguards to protect your information, but no system is perfectly secure.

Your choices

  • Update or delete your profile information from the app settings.
  • Disable optional permissions like location in your device settings.
  • Request account deletion by emailing support.
